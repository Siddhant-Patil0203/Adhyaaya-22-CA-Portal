Adhyaaya, GCOEN is one of the largest Science and Technology festivals in Maharashtra, and the CA Program is undoubtedly its backbone. We at Adhyaaya, aspire to reach out to college students across the globe to spark their interest in Science and Technology and provide them a platform to pursue the same. The CA Program helps us realize this vision. Here, the CAs build a network of students from 500+ Indian colleges and learn about social media, marketing, event planning and execution. They are rewarded for fulfilling their responsibilities and motivated to strive to improve their personal and professional skills continually. The CA program last year was a great success and we hope to continue the great work this year, too. As Adhyaaya celebrates its fifth iteration this year, the role of the CAs is more important than ever before and this is a golden opportunity for all of us to work together and make the 5th Adhyaaya a grand success, bigger and better than ever before.

Role of Campus Ambassador

Act as a connecting link

Represent Adhyaaya at your college, and act as a link between the students of the respective college and Adhyaaya.

Planning and execution

Ideate, plan, organize, and promote the events of Adhyaaya through various mediums and channels

Connect & Engage

FAQs

Am I eligible to be a college ambassador?

All you need is your college ID and lot of enthusiasm, and a will to explore a new experience.

How can I apply for the post of campus ambassador?

Visit Adhyaaya 2022’s CA portal-> (link), click on the ‘Register’ button, you will be redirected to a google form, fill it thoroughly and boom! You’re registered!

How will my progress be monitored?

You will upload the proof of your work, our team will verify it and reward you with points, which will be a measure of your progress. You can contact your CA executives, in case of any queries.

What do I get for being an ambassador?

We have a lot of rewards in store for you, depending on their performance each participant will get exciting rewards like goodies, courses, etc.

ABOUT US

ADHYAAYA 2021 is the fourth iteration of the national level technical symposium of Government College of Engineering, Nagpur. Students from all over Maharashtra set their keen eyes on Adhyaaya. Last year, more than 3500 budding technocrats from all across India marked their remarkable presence in Adhyaaya'20. The event consists of various technical, non-technical segments along with workshops on topics spread all over the diverse scopes of engineering. We strive to offer a platform for the student community to develop and showcase their skill sets and technical prowess by presenting them events from model presentations, code-hunts to e-sports and technical workshops. Adhyaaya is considered to be a promising technical fest in Nagpur and an experience to be remembered. So mark your calendars! Adhyaaya 2021 begins this 13th of May 2021. We appreciate your presence and our journey together.
